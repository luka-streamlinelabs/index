Coach's Notes

Things to remember this summer

AM fasted training ceiling
Going in fully fasted limits your performance ceiling on heavy compound lifts. Even 30g of carbs (banana) makes a measurable difference. Don't be a hero — eat the fruit.
PM pre-workout hits harder
A fed state means better blood flow, higher glycogen, and pre-workout ingredients absorb more effectively. If you're choosing a schedule for a heavy leg or pressing day, PM wins every time.
Creatine saturation takes 2 weeks
Once saturated (after ~14 days of consistent daily dosing) the timing stops mattering. What matters is not missing days. Set an alarm if you have to.
Growth timing check
If Growth contains ZMA (zinc + magnesium aspartate), the label will say take it before bed on an empty stomach. Calcium in food blocks zinc absorption. Check the label and shift accordingly.
Hydrate scoop strategy
Split ½ pre / ½ intra for sessions 60–75 min. Use a full scoop pre-workout for shorter sessions (<45 min). For 90+ min summer outdoor sessions, consider 1.5 scoops total.
Post-workout window is real
30–45 min post-training is your highest MPS window. Don't shower, scroll, and forget. Have the shake ready to go before you start training so there's zero friction post-session.
Protein targets — summer build
At 85kg targeting lean hypertrophy: aim for 170–190g protein daily (2g/kg). Your post-workout shake covers ~30g. Lunch and dinner need to carry 40–50g each. Breakfast fills the rest.
